# CAP-aligned Message Templates (Text)

These templates help you draft consistent “do/don’t” messages that can be mapped into CAP fields.

## Message 1 — Initial status update (low panic)
Title: Situation under assessment (do not spread unverified information)
Body:
- We are aware of reports of [incident type] near [approx area].
- Verification is in progress with [site operator / security contact].
- Please avoid sharing unverified claims. If you have direct evidence, submit it via [report channel].
Action checklist:
- Do: keep distance, follow site staff instructions, report evidence
- Don’t: click unknown links, share “official notices” from unknown sources

## Message 2 — Anti-fraud clarification (phishing containment)
Title: Fake “official notice” circulating — do not click
Body:
- A message claiming to be an official evacuation notice is circulating with a suspicious link.
- Do not click or forward. Use only confirmed official channels: [list your approved sources].
Action checklist:
- Do: report the message, delete it, warn contacts using safe wording
- Don’t: enter credentials, install apps, scan unknown QR codes

## Message 3 — Final update + education
Title: Update: [resolved/ongoing] + how to report effectively
Body:
- Current status: [brief, factual].
- What we learned: [1–3 bullets].
- Next time, include: time, approximate area, clear photo (if safe), and what you observed.

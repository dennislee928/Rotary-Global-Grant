# Sponsor Checklist (For Rotary Meetings)

## What to bring
- One pager: `docs/00_project_brief_one_pager.md`
- CNA plan (Taipei + Glasgow)
- KPI/M&E plan
- Pilot playbooks (tabletop + drill)
- University offer/programme details
- Scholar profile (1 page) + CV

## Questions to answer clearly
- Which Area of Focus is primary and why?
- Who benefits and how will you measure it?
- What are safeguarding/abuse controls?
- What is the pilot scope and timeline?
- What does the host sponsor do (events, introductions, local validation)?
- How will Rotary relationship continue post-scholarship?
